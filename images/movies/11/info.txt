Grossed: $1.347 Billion
Black Panther was released in 2018 and is an American Superhero Film about a Marvel comic book character of the same name. 

The film has grossed $1.347 billion dollars to date and was produced by Marvel Studios, distributed by Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures and was directed by Ryan Coogler. 

If you’re unfamiliar with the Black Panther then the film is about the King of Wakanda, T’Challa’s, mission to stop his adversary abandoning his country’s traditional policies in favour of a world revolution. 

Black Panther received several industry nominations and was the first MCU film to win an academy award. 

If you’re into this film, then you’ll be pleased to know that the sequel will be released in 2022, written and directed by Ryan Coogler again.
