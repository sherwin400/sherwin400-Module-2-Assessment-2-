Grossed: $1.519 Billion
Released in 2012, the Avengers has grossed £1.519 billion dollars to date. 

It’s an American superhero film, based on the Marvel comics and is the sixth film to be released in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU).

The film was written and directed by Joss Whedon, produced by Marvel Studios and distributed by Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures. 

In this Avengers film, S.H.I.E.L.D boss, Nick Fury, recruits the Avengers to locate and stop Thor’s brother Loki from destroying Earth. 

The film set and broke and a number of records; including the biggest opening weekend in the United States and Canada, and became the highest-grossing film of 2012. 

It’s also the first-ever Marvel film to break the $1 billion mark in ticket sales!
