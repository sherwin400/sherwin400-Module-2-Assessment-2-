Grossed: $2.79 Billion
In second place we have a film that’s grossed over $2.79 billion dollars and broke several box office awards: Avatar.  

The film was released in 2009 and is a science fiction film, directed, written and co-edited and James Cameron. 




Avatars cast includes Sam Worthington, Zoe Saldana, Stephen Lang, Michelle Rodriguez, and Sigourney Weaver.

The film follows a group of humans as they try to colonise another planet, only to find out that it already has a native local tribe, the Na’vi, who are a humanoid species that are indigenous to the planet, Pandora. 

As well as becoming the highest-grossing film at the time it was released, Avatar also remained the highest-grossing film worldwide for almost a decade. 

Also, after being nominated for nine Academy Awards, Avatar won three, including Best Art Direction, Best Cinematography and Best Visual Effects.
